Common Questions

Do I need a referral?

No referral needed! You can schedule directly. We offer free consultations to determine if Emsella is right for you.

Is it covered by insurance?

Unfortunately, even though it is an FDA-cleared device for treating incontinence and other pelvic floor issues, it is not an insurance-covered service. However, you can use your HSA (Health Savings Account) card to pay for treatment. Note: FSA cards typically cannot be used, but HSA cards are accepted. We also offer payment plans to make treatment more accessible.

How do I schedule?

Call us at (414) 295-6045 to schedule your free consultation. We'll discuss your concerns, answer questions, and determine if Emsella is the right solution for you.

What if I'm embarrassed to call?

We completely understand. Incontinence is a sensitive topic, but know that we treat this every day. Our staff is professional, compassionate, and discreet. You'll be treated with dignity and respect from your first call through your last session. If you prefer, you can also text us at (414) 295-6045 to schedule or ask questions.
